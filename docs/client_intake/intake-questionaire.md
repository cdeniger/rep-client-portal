2.	Who is your current employer?
3.	What industry or domain best describes your work?
4.	Approximately how many years of professional experience do you have?
o	5–7
o	8–10
o	11–15
o	16+
5.	Are you currently employed?
o	Yes
o	No
o	Consulting / interim
 
SECTION 2 — Career Direction & Trajectory
Purpose: Define where your career is meant to go — not just the next move.
6.	Which statement best describes how you want your career to evolve?
(Select one primary; optional secondary)
o	Increasing scope of ownership inside companies
o	General management / P&L ownership
o	Executive leadership (VP / C-suite track)
o	Platform leadership (advisor, board, operating partner)
o	Specialist authority (rare, high-leverage expert)
o	Founder / builder (not assumed)
7.	Over the long term (5–10 years), which outcomes do you most want your work to be judged on?
(Select up to 3)
o	Revenue growth
o	Cost efficiency
o	Risk management
o	Team scale
o	Strategic direction
o	Market credibility / reputation
o	Wealth creation
8.	Which of the following do you not want your career optimized for?
(Critical signal — select any that apply)
o	Constant job switching
o	Narrow specialization
o	People management
o	Political leadership roles
o	Geographic mobility
o	Public visibility
 
SECTION 3 — Time Horizon & Decision Framing
Purpose: Anchor decisions to time, not emotion.
9.	Ideally, how long would you want your next role to last?
o	Less than 18 months
o	2–3 years
o	3–5 years
o	Open-ended
10.	At what point should your next role clearly “pay off” from a career perspective?
•	Immediately (primarily compensation-driven)
•	Within 12–18 months
•	Within 2–3 years
•	Long-term (5+ years)
11.	When you think about career progress, which lens matters most to you?
•	Title progression
•	Scope progression
•	Economic participation
•	Optionality / freedom
 
SECTION 4 — Ownership Scope (Current & Desired)
Purpose: Quantify ownership — not responsibilities.
12.	Today, what are you directly accountable for?
(Select all that apply)
•	Revenue
•	Costs / budget
•	P&L
•	Headcount
•	Strategic roadmap
•	Regulatory / risk exposure
•	None of the above
13.	At your next level, what should you be accountable for?
•	Same scope, larger scale
•	First-time ownership of one or more areas above
•	Full business line or function
•	Enterprise-wide scope
14.	Which ownership dimension do you most want to expand next?
•	Financial outcomes
•	People & org design
•	Strategic direction
•	External stakeholders
 
SECTION 5 — Decision Authority & Risk Posture
Purpose: Differentiate “trusted operator” from “decision owner.”
15.	In your current role, how are decisions typically made?
•	I decide independently
•	I recommend; others decide
•	Decisions are consensus-driven
•	Decisions are largely pre-set
16.	In the future, which decisions do you want authority over?
(Select all that apply)
•	Hiring / firing
•	Budget allocation
•	Strategic priorities
•	Vendor / partner selection
•	Product / roadmap direction
17.	How comfortable are you being visibly accountable for decisions that fail?
•	Very comfortable
•	Comfortable with support
•	Prefer shared accountability
•	Prefer to avoid visible failure risk
 
SECTION 6 — Compensation Orientation
Purpose: Understand how money should function in your career — not just lifestyle needs.
18.	Over the next 5–10 years, which matters most?
•	Maximizing total earnings
•	Maximizing flexibility / optionality
•	Maximizing authority and influence
19.	How do you feel about non-linear compensation (equity, upside-heavy structures)?
•	Comfortable with equity-heavy upside
•	Prefer predictable cash
•	Will trade cash for long-term leverage
•	Unsure / want guidance
20.	What signals “financial success” to you?
(Select up to 2)
•	Annual income
•	Equity events
•	Net worth milestones
•	Ability to say no to work
•	Time freedom
 
SECTION 7 — Market Identity & Visibility
Purpose: Control how the market categorizes you without explanation.
21.	How do you believe the market currently categorizes you?
•	Strong executor
•	Emerging leader
•	Strategic operator
•	Functional specialist
•	Not sure
22.	How do you want the market to categorize you in ~5 years?
•	Owner of outcomes
•	General manager
•	Trusted executive
•	Industry authority
23.	Are you interested in building visible market presence?
(Select any that apply)
•	Thought leadership
•	Advisory roles
•	Board exposure
•	Speaking / writing
•	Prefer low visibility
 
SECTION 8 — Assets (Inference-First Inputs)
Purpose: Let Rep. analyze signal directly rather than relying on self-reporting.
24.	Please share a link to your LinkedIn profile.
25.	Please upload your current resume.
